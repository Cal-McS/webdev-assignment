Bo'ness Town

Brief Introduction

Bo'ness (Borrowstounness) is a historic town located in the central belt of Scotland. The town doesn't have an exact foundation date, however the name Bo'ness is believes to have been in circulation since the 1400s. The origin of the name Borrowstounness comes from "Borrowstoun" which comes from the Old English for "Beornweard's farmstead" and "ness" which comes from the Scottish Gaelic for "headland".
